# Billing FAQ (sample SaaS docs)

## Payment methods
We accept credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express) and bank transfer
for annual invoices over $1,200. ACH is available for US customers. Crypto and
PayPal are not currently supported.

## When am I charged?
For monthly plans you are charged on the same day of the month as your initial
signup. Annual plans are charged once on the signup anniversary. We send a
receipt by email after every successful charge. You can also download receipts
from Settings → Billing → Receipts.

## Adding or removing seats
You can add or remove seats at any time from the Admin Console. Adding seats
prorates and charges the difference immediately. Removing seats credits the
unused portion to your account; the credit is applied to your next renewal.

## Upgrading or downgrading
Upgrading takes effect immediately and prorates the cost. Downgrading takes
effect at the end of your current billing period. Annual customers who downgrade
keep their higher-tier features until the annual period ends, then move to the
new tier on renewal.

## Tax
We charge tax where required by law (including US state sales tax, GST/HST in
Canada, VAT in the EU and UK, and GST in Australia). Your invoice shows the
breakdown. To add or update a tax ID, go to Settings → Billing → Tax.

## Discounts and credits
Annual billing receives a 17% discount versus the equivalent monthly plan; the
discount is applied automatically at checkout. Account credits from seat
removals or refunds appear on your next invoice as a deduction. We don't
issue promo codes outside of campaigns.
